A little look inside me

Painted walls with blood and ink

With everything gone i still think about everything that torned us apart... together apart,
Nothing will erase this things from my head, nothing will heal the big whole that you left me in.
What can a simple mortal do...? nothing but look at it and smile, smile while i can.
This aint a goodbye neither something close to one see you later
This is just me talking to that wall painted with blood and ink.

Can we discuss something about our past? something so burried inside ourself that not even our brightest psicologist could bring it up?
unseen by this rooted society, by this heartless people? can we make it up to all these years lost in our thoughts?
I believe not... there's nothing to remember, there's nothing to heal... you take it all you stole from me everything you could imagine and more.
Dont tell me now that you going to regret for taking take a step away to breath... cuz you take the only thing that i wanted to breathe.
And it was you the one who left... the one who make this... together apart.
Together apart...

Twisted like a twister.

There is a bit of me in you, and there's a bit of you in everything i can think of.
There's a meaning between lines... you just have to be carefull to read'em right.
With all the will to live our life we just walk right directly to our certain death.
i told someone that i didn't love you at all, then they asked me if i have missed you? and i told them... I still try
a Twisted mind like myself will always have something to say.
